Methodological support

Methodological support of the educational process is carried out by the forces of the graduating and servicing subject-cycle commissions, which offer students in hard copy or electronic form:

- materials for classroom work in each discipline of the curriculum, including lecture abstracts, practical lesson plans, etc.;

- materials for students' independent work, including self-control materials for each discipline, training tests, control tasks, exam questions for each discipline;

- materials for practical training, including plans and programs of practice for all types, forms of accounting documentation.
