Organization of student's leisure time

The Youth Affairs Committee organizes students' leisure activities.


"Being young means 'running' forward.

To run, creating, helping loved ones, leaving a positive trace behind.

Study, strive, be patriotic and think about the future!"


Our youth should live with dignity and, like the apple of their eye, they should cherish the customs and traditions of the people, learn and inspire others with their skills and talents, be free and implement the advanced humane ideas of humanity.

Committee on Youth Affairs (KDM) is a structural unit of the college in the field of implementation of the state youth policy of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

The main objectives of KDM's activities:

- activation of students' social activities;

- formation of the civic consciousness of youth;

- fostering patriotic feelings;

- support and implementation of student initiatives;

- promoting the development of personal self-realization of students.

The primary tasks of the KDM's activities:

- education of an educated, active and conscious citizen, a patriot of his country;

- protection of the rights and legitimate interests of college students;

- the development of an independent, harmoniously developed creative personality capable of adapting to the changing conditions of society;

- promoting the broad participation of students in social, cultural and public activities of the State;

- providing support to talented and gifted youth;

- creating conditions for dialogue and interaction between students and the college administration;

- assistance in the formation of a healthy lifestyle among students.


AREAS OF EDUCATIONAL WORK


Direction Explanation
Education of new Kazakhstani patriotism and citizenship, legal education. the formation of a patriot and a citizen with a rational and emotional attitude to the motherland, the need to assimilate and comply with the laws of the state and society, opposing political, legal and anti-corruption lawlessness, ready to resist manifestations of cruelty and violence among children and youth.
Spiritual and moral education formation of a deep understanding of the value foundations of the "Rukhani Zhangyru" program on the revival of spiritual, moral and ethical principles of a person, her moral qualities and attitudes consistent with universal human values, norms and traditions of the life of Kazakhstani society.
National education the orientation of the individual towards universal and national values, respect for the native and state languages, culture of the Kazakh people, ethnic groups and ethnic groups of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
Family education educating parents, increasing their psychological and pedagogical competence and responsibility for the upbringing of children. Family Day
Labor, economic and environmental education. the formation of a conscious attitude to professional self-determination, the development of economic thinking and environmental culture of the individual.
Multicultural and artistic and aesthetic education the formation of general cultural behavioral skills, the development of a person's readiness to perceive, master, and evaluate aesthetic objects in art and reality, the creation of a multicultural environment in educational organizations.
Intellectual education, education of information culture. formation of a motivational space that ensures the development of intellectual capabilities, leadership qualities and giftedness of each individual, as well as information culture.
Physical education, healthy lifestyle. creating a space for the successful formation of healthy lifestyle skills, maintaining physical and psychological health, and the ability to identify factors that are harmful to health.
