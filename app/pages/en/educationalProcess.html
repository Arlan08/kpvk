Organization of the educational process

1. General provisions

The organization of the educational process at the college in full-time and part-time education is carried out in accordance with the Law of the Republic of Kazakhstan "On Education" and is regulated by working curricula, the schedule of the educational process, the schedule of training sessions in specialties.

Working curricula have been developed in accordance with the state mandatory standard of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

The main types of full-time training are: lesson, lecture, seminar, practical lesson, laboratory work, control work, independent work, consultation, practice, course work.

The academic year at the full-time department begins on September 1 and ends according to the schedule of the educational process for 2021-2022 academic year (full-time study) and the schedule of the educational process for 2021-2022 academic year (distance learning). Students' educational, industrial, technological and pre-graduate practice is conducted in institutions and organizations on the basis of social partnership agreements concluded between the college and organizations.

Full-time students have the right to:

- to receive high-quality education in accordance with the State Mandatory Standard of Technical and Vocational Education of the Republic of Kazakhstan, current working curricula;

- to receive additional, including paid educational services;

For failure to fulfill the work curriculum in the specialty within the established time frame for a disrespectful reason, failure to fulfill the duties provided for by the college charter, violation of internal regulations, disciplinary penalties may be applied to students up to expulsion from the college.
