Information and library support of the educational process

1. General provisions

The library is designed to meet the information needs of the main contingent of readers: students and college staff.

The library is designed to provide effective training for students in the framework of educational programs of all specialties.

The main document giving readers the right to use the library is a student ID.

When registering for the library, the reader should familiarize himself with the Rules of Use of the library.

The Library's schedule is posted at the entrance to the library.


2. The rights, duties and responsibilities of readers

Library readers have the right to:

- to use the main types of library and information services provided by the library;

- to receive full information about the composition of the library fund through the catalog and other forms of library information;

- receive from the library fund for temporary use in the reading room and on subscription of any printed works;

- to receive advisory assistance in the search and selection of works of print.

Responsibilities of readers:

- follow these rules;

- be polite and friendly towards the library staff;

- take care of publications received from the library's collection:

- return it within the established time frame;

- do not take them out of the library premises unless they are recorded in the reader's form or other accounting documents;

- do not make notes, underscores, tear out or fold pages in them;

- upon receipt of books and other materials, the reader must carefully look at them and, if any defects are found, inform the librarian about it, otherwise the reader who used the book last is responsible for spoiling the books;

- Upon leaving the college, readers must return the publications listed in the library.

Responsibility of the reader:

- the reader responsible for the loss or unintentional damage of 1 printed publication is obliged to replace it with 5 of the same publications recognized by the library as equivalent, and if replacement is impossible, reimburse the commercial value of the publications five times;

- for repeated violations of the Rules of Use, the reader may be excluded from the library for up to 6 months;

- for intentional damage to publications, the reader is deprived of the right to use the library.

Library Responsibilities:

- To inform readers about all types of services provided by the library.

- To provide readers with the opportunity to use all the information resources of the library.

- To improve library and information and bibliographic services for readers by introducing computerization and new information technologies.

- To provide a high culture of service: to assist readers in choosing the necessary publications; to organize book exhibitions and other events; to be attentive and friendly to readers;

- To create and maintain comfortable working conditions for the reader in the library.

The order of writing readers to the library:

- The student ID is the only document granting the right to use the library;

- When signing up for the library, the reader should familiarize himself with the Rules of Use of the library;

- A reader's form is a document certifying the fact of issuing publications at home and returning them to the library.

The order of use of the reading room:

- When ordering printed publications in the reading room, the reader presents a student or library card and signs the book forms upon receipt of the publications.

- Printed publications issued to the reading room from the book depository can be reserved for a certain period of time.

- Encyclopedias, reference books, rare and valuable publications, the latest or only copies of publications are issued only in the reading room.

- It is forbidden to take out publications from the reading room.

- Before leaving the library, the reader must hand over all publications received in the reading room.


3. Subscription usage rules

To order and receive books on a subscription, readers present a library card, a correctly filled out reader's requirement and sign on the book form.

When issuing publications, the librarian receives a library card, finds a reader's form, makes sure that the painting on the book form is correct and puts it in the reader's form.

The issue of educational literature is carried out for a semester.

Readers can extend the period of use of home-borrowed publications if there is no demand for them from other readers.

A reader who has not extended the period of use of publications is a debtor and pays a fine.

Additional information about educational literature can be obtained from the college library.
